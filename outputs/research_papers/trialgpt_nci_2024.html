Highlights

  • An end-to-end framework for zero-shot patient-to-trial matching with LLM
  • Comprises three modules: TrialGPT-Retrieval, TrialGPT-Matching, and TrialGPT-Ranking
  • Achieves an accuracy of 87.3% with faithful explanations, close to the expert performance
  • Reduces the screening time by 42.6% in patient recruitment
